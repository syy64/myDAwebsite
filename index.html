About

Behind the analysis.

I work with data, but my focus is always the decision behind it. With a foundation in economics and hands-on experience in data analysis, I approach every problem by asking the right questions first - then using data to find clear, practical answers that people can act on.

"Numbers don't speak for themselves. My job is to make them say the right thing - clearly, and in language that drives action."

I've built dashboards, analysed business performance, and worked with real operational data. My economics background gives me a lens for forecasting, risk thinking, and market patterns - not just what the data says, but what it means for the business. Every deliverable I produce is built around one question: what decision does this need to support?

Research & Analysis Approach

I apply structured research thinking to every project, framing hypotheses before opening a dataset, stress-testing assumptions, and documenting reasoning. It's what separates analysis that informs from analysis that just reports.
